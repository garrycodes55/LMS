Case Study 1

Wabtec corporation is a large transportation company that manufactures diesel locomotives. They are considering entering the battery electric locomotives & hydrogen fueled locomotives market. Should they enter?​

  • Type of Case Study:- Market entry ​
  • Expectations/Learning Outcomes:- ​ ​

1. Understand why the company wants to enter the market.
​ 2. Quantify the specific target or goal.​
3. Develop a market entry framework and work through the case.​
4. Consider the market entry strategy OR consider alternatives to entering the market.​
5. Deliver a recommendation and propose next steps.
​ ​

  • Benefits​

LEADs: 1. Case studies improve their business acumen.


2. Build their research, teamwork and presentation skills. ​


WABTEC: : 1. New ideas from the young minds. ​


2. Emerging leaders with business and technical acumen.​ ​
